What are culturally safe practices for discussing end-of-life care with an Indigenous patient?
EduCareAI · Clinical Guideline
Community & Family
Engage family and community early — collective decision-making is central in many Indigenous cultures. Do not assume individual autonomy as the default framework.
Cultural Navigation
Involve an Indigenous Patient Navigator or Elder where available. Follow the patient's lead on spiritual and ceremonial needs throughout the process.
Language & Framing
Frame care around relationships, land, and continuity rather than clinical endpoints. Avoid language that centres individual autonomy alone.
Deference Protocol
When in doubt, pause and defer to the patient's community context before proceeding. Document cultural preferences alongside clinical notes.
